Scale-Bearing Merchant
  • I wish to have great strength - Raises Strength in exchange for all other stats
  • I wish to have great dexterity - Raises Dexterity in exchange for all other stats
  • I wish to expand my intellect - Raises Intelligence in exchange for all other stats
  • I wish to have deep faith - Raises Faith in exchange for all other stats
  • I wish to be in touch with the arcane - Raises Arcane in exchange for all other stats
  • I want resistance to ailments - Increases Resistances by +150, but lose 10% Stamina
  • I want to hold death at bay - Lose 20% max HP. When HP drops to 20% or lower, immediately heal back to 100% HP, once per battle. Does not trigger against damage that instantly kills you from above 20% HP.
  • I desire a flask - Adds a Flask Charge but lose 10% max HP.
    • Take sacred flask - A variant of the above, but without any cost. This is only available if the run was invaded and Libra was paid off.
  • I want the power of a demon - Grants an Eye that intermittently shoots enemies for small amount of damage and Madness buildup, or occasionally grants you a 10% attack boost for 10 seconds. It builds up Madness whenever it appears.
  • I desire a powerful weapon - Lose 2 levels but randomly get one of the following: Vyke's War Spear, Black Knife, Erdtree Bow, Frenzied Flame Seal, Fingerprint Stone Shield, Miquellan Knight's Sword, Ordovis's Greatsword, Halo Scythe. Passive effects are determined randomly.
    • Take enhanced armament - A variant of the above, but without any level cost. This is only available if the run was invaded and Libra was paid off.
  • I wish for eventual greatness - Take a -30% HP, FP, and Stamina debuff that lasts for the rest of the day. After that debuff expires at the start of the next day, gain a +20% HP, FP, and Stamina buff permanently. If you die before any of that happens, all aspects of this deal are cancelled.
    • Take improved physique - A variant of the above, but without any debuff. This is only available if the run was invaded and Libra was paid off.
  • I want a lot of runes - Places the Symbol of Avarice in a vacant talisman slot, at the cost of 10,000 Runes. If both talisman slots are filled, you have to pick which talisman to drop in order to receive it. Available only during days 1 and 2. If the run was invaded and Libra was paid off, you do not pay any runes.
