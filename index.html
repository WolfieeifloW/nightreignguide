Stonesword Key Possible Locations
  • Broken Carriages:
    • Check in the back of broken down carriages

  • Great Churches:
    • Up in rafters
    • Behind 'altar' on ground level
    • If there is a hole:
      • Underground past boss in right room

  • Forts
    • Roof, top of left or right tower beside top of stairs
    • Roof, room where map is
    • 'Go under' type fort:
      • Second floor, right room
      • Roof, chests beneath stairs
    • 'Front stairs' type fort:
      • Bottom floor, back room
      • Roof, chest in back
DAY 1
  • Primary Objectives
    • Do landing camp and level to 2
    • ONLY get Churches that are not out of the way
    • Get a better weapon
    • Should be level 7+
    • Center castle: basement red eye boss, talisman from middle
      • If it's trolls it may be better to leave and do field bosses

  • Secondary Objectives
    • Clear Forts & Great Churches for Stonesword Keys
    • Open Evergaol's, even if you can't complete them
    • Get a Smithing Stone 2 (can be done on Day 2 too)
    • Getting Talismans (treasure goblins)

  • Field Bosses:
    • SAFE: Leonine, Lion, Red Wolf, Miranda flower, Hippo, Black Knife Asssassin, Demi-Human Queen

DAY 2
  • Primary Objectives
    • Can do shifting earth zone on Day 2
    • Kill field bosses for good XP
    • Should be level 10+

  • Secondary Objectives
    • Anything you didn't get done on Day 1

RANDOM INFO
  • You get ~14 minutes per day
  • Top of fort has map to mark treasure goblins
  • Get Evergaol key from Forts or Great Churches
    • Evergaol bosses scale with time (not level)
  • Run past small enemies, don't waste time fighting them

Mage Towers
  • If there is a door:
    • Misty door w/ no symbol = fake door
    • Sigil above door = attack it
    • Shack = left side of tower and attack
    • Dead trees on left side = attack trees
    • Imp statue = attack it
    • Invisible crest = pond reveals location
    • Rotting tree stump = chase and kill
    • Shaking trees = attack and kill
  • If there is no door:
    • Extinguished candle = attack it
    • If candle is lit, hit back right wall
